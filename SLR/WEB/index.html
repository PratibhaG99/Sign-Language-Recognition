Dissolving Differences


Language should be a medium, not a barrier!

How many of us have often thought of communicating with the specially-abled community but felt handicapped due to this huge language barrier between these two sections?
NOT ANYMORE!
Our Vision is the familiarization of Hindi as a linguistic language for the specially-abled kids of our country. Not just kids but people from every walk of society will be able to learn the Indian Sign Language through our website.

You are just a CLICK away to dive into the world of learning and connect with the feelings of your loved ones much more deeply than ever. Don't worry the process is hassle free and you can enjoy this journey at your own pace.
